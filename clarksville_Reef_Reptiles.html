Welcome to Clarksville Reef & Reptiles

A retail pet store in Clarksville, Tennessee!

Since 2015, we are the leading pet store for aquatic and reptile needs servicing Clarksville, Oak Grove, Hopkinsville, Ashland City, Pleasant View and the surrounding areas. From salt and fresh water fish to snakes, lizards, scorpions and more, we have all you will need to provide the best care and home for your family pet! Our services include reef and reptile tank repairs and installs, tank set up and maintenance, tear downs and moving of tanks.
